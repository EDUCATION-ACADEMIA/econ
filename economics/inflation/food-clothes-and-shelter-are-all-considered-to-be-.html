Food, clothes and shelter are all considered to be ____

(A) urges

(B) wants

(C) ** needs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A traditional list of immediate “basic needs” is food (including water), shelter and clothing.

Concept note-2: -Food to eat, clothing to wear, and shelter from the elements. Aside from clothing, food and shelter are the same basic needs that every non-swimming living thing on this planet requires. Without sustenance and a place to sleep and protect oneself from environmental conditions, it is almost impossible to live.

Concept note-3: -The above-given statement is true. Ninety-five percent of our basic needs for food, shelter, and clothing are obtained from the land. Because the land is the way to the natural resource, which is important for livelihood, and this is available in the form of forest land, mountain ecosystem, riverine system, etc.

Concept note-4: -We must have food, water, air, and shelter to survive. If any one of these basic needs is not met, then humans cannot survive.

Concept note-5: -Answer: We need clothes to cover ourselves and protect us from Dust, rain, insects, sunlight, etc. Like food and shelter are basic needs of a human being to survive.
