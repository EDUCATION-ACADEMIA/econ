If the Price of the Market Basket in 2008 is $40 and the Price of the Market Basket in the base year is $20, what is the CPI for 2008?

(A) 2

(B) 60

(C) 80

(D) ** 200

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To find the CPI in any year, divide the cost of the market basket in year t by the cost of the same market basket in the base year. The CPI in 1984 = $75/$75 x 100 = 100 The CPI is just an index value and it is indexed to 100 in the base year, in this case 1984.
