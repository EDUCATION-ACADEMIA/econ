PROFIT

[SOURCES]
Average revenue (AR) is the same thing as

(A) ** price per unit

(B) TR/Output

(C) the extra revenue received as a result of selling one more unit of output

(D) Total revenue less marginal revenue

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Average revenue is the revenue earned per unit of output sold in the market. It is identical to the price of the output produced.

Concept note-2: -Thus, AR is always equal to price.

Concept note-3: -Average revenue = Total revenue / quantity of units or users. Revenue refers to all the money a company earns during a specific time period. Companies can calculate valuable information about revenue when they use the average revenue formula, which is like finding the mathematical average of any set of numbers.

Concept note-4: -Average revenue (AR) and price always equal under all market forms. As we know that AR is equal to per unit sale receipts and price is always per unit. This is because the sellers receive revenue according to price.

Concept note-5: -Average Revenue refers to the revenue per unit of output sold. Thus, it is proved that AR = Price.
