There are several remedies for negative consumption externalities, including imposing ____ and setting ____ prices, imposing fines for ____ consumption, controlling supply through a licensing system.

(A) ** indirect taxes, minimum, over

(B) indirect taxes, maximum, under

(C) direct taxes, limited, over

(D) taxes, minimum, over

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Remedies for Negative Externalities One of the solutions to negative externalities is to impose taxes to change peopleâ€™s behavior. The taxes can be imposed to reduce the harmful effects of certain externalities such as air pollution, smoking, and drinking alcohol.

Concept note-2: -A negative externality exists when the production or consumption of a product results in a cost to a third party. Air and noise pollution are commonly cited examples of negative externalities.

Concept note-3: -A Pigovian tax is intended to tax the producer of goods or services that create adverse side effects for society. Economists argue that the costs of these negative externalities, such as environmental pollution, are borne by society rather than the producer.
