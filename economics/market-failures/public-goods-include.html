Public goods include

(A) banking and other financial services.

(B) child-care services.

(C) computer repair services.

(D) ** fire and police services.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In economics, a public good refers to a commodity or service that is made available to all members of a society. Typically, these services are administered by governments and paid for collectively through taxation. Examples of public goods include law enforcement, national defense, and the rule of law.

Concept note-2: -2. Public parks, streetlight, police protection are examples of Public goods or services.

Concept note-3: -Examples of goods under-provided include merit goods and public goods. Fire service could be considered a public good. Because fire prevention and fire extinguishing services share the characteristics of public goods.

Concept note-4: -The types of public goods include security, education, knowledge, infrastructure, environment and health.

Concept note-5: -The government plays a significant role in providing goods such as national defence, infrastructure, education, security, and fire and environmental protection almost everywhere. These goods are often referred to as “public goods”.
