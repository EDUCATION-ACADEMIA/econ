What does the UAE export to Japan?

(A) Cars

(B) Electronics

(C) ** Oil

(D) All answers are correct

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -(¥3.16B). In December 2022 the top imports of Japan from United Arab Emirates were Crude Petroleum (¥255B), Petroleum Gas (¥36.4B), Refined Petroleum (¥34.7B), Raw Aluminium (¥19.7B), and Precious Metal Scraps (¥1.52B).

Concept note-2: -The main products that United Arab Emirates exported to Japan are Crude Petroleum ($12.1B), Refined Petroleum ($1.29B), and Petroleum Gas ($540M). During the last 25 years the exports of United Arab Emirates to Japan have increased at an annualized rate of 2.23%, from $8.78B in 1995 to $15.2B in 2020.

Concept note-3: -The main destination of Refined Petroleum exports from United Arab Emirates are: Iraq ($2.35B), Pakistan ($2.21B), South Korea ($1.55B), Singapore ($1.41B), and Japan ($1.29B).

Concept note-4: -At the same year, Crude Petroleum was the 1st most exported product in United Arab Emirates. The main destination of Crude Petroleum exports from United Arab Emirates are: Japan ($12.1B), China ($8.6B), India ($6.34B), Thailand ($3.84B), and South Korea ($3.18B).
