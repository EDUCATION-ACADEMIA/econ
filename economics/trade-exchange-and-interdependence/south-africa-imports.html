South Africa imports:

(A) Minerals (Gold, Iron, Diamonds)

(B) ** Computers

(C) Fruit

(D) ** Special Medicines

(E) ** Machines

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -South Africa main imports are: machinery (21.3 percent of total imports), mineral products (16.9 percent), chemicals (12.6 percent) and vehicles and aircraft vessels (7.7 percent). Main trading partners are China, India and the US.

Concept note-2: -The following products are growing in popularity as South Africa’s most sought-after exports, and would be great additions to your inventory: South African wines. Fruits and nuts. Clothing and footwear.

Concept note-3: -Mineral fuels including oil: US$15.4 billion (16.5% of total imports) Machinery including computers: $11.3 billion (12.1%) Electrical machinery, equipment: $8.6 billion (9.2%) Vehicles: $6.4 billion (6.8%) Pharmaceuticals: $3.1 billion (3.3%) Plastics, plastic articles: $2.9 billion (3.1%) More items •04-Oct-2022
