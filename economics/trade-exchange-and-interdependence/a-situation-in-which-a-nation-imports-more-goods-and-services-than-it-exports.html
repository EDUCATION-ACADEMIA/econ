A situation in which a nation imports more goods and services than it exports.

(A) Balance of Trade

(B) Balance of Payments

(C) Trade Surplus

(D) ** Trade Deficit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A trade deficit occurs when a countryâ€™s imports exceed its exports during a given time period. It is also referred to as a negative balance of trade (BOT).

Concept note-2: -A country has a trade deficit when the value of its imports exceeds the value of its exports. The impacts of trade deficits are frequently over-simplified. Trade deficits can be damaging but they also bring welcome economic benefits.

Concept note-3: -A country that imports more goods and services than it exports in terms of value has a trade deficit or a negative trade balance.

Concept note-4: -A trade deficit occurs when a nation imports more than it exports.

Concept note-5: -If the value of exports exceeds the value of imports, it is said that there is a trade surplus; if imports are greater than exports, the country has a trade deficit.
