Corporations such as Ford Motor Company, Shell Oil Company, and British Petroleum, which produce and sell products in different countries, are called

(A) ** Multinational

(B) Mega companies

(C) Industries

(D) Globals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A multinational corporation (MNC) is usually a large corporation incorporated in one country which produces or sells goods or services in various countries.

Concept note-2: -There are four types of multinational companies: decentralised multinational corporations, global centralised corporations, international companies, and transnational enterprises.

Concept note-3: -multinational corporation (MNC), also called transnational corporation, any corporation that is registered and operates in more than one country at a time. Generally the corporation has its headquarters in one country and operates wholly or partially owned subsidiaries in other countries.

Concept note-4: -What Are Examples of Multinational Corporations? Examples of multinational corporations include Apple, Amazon, Microsoft, McDonaldâ€™s, and Volkswagen. These companies are headquartered in one nation but operate divisions in many other countries in order to expand their business and reach more customers.

Concept note-5: -MNCs have production and marketing operations in several countries; operating through a network of branches, subsidiaries and affiliates in host countries. MNCs are characterized by unity of control. MNCs control business activities of their branches in foreign countries through head office located in the home country.
