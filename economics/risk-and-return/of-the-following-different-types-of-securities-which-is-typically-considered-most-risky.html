Of the following different types of securities, which is typically considered most risky?

(A) long-term corporate bonds

(B) long-term government bonds

(C) common stocks of large companies

(D) ** common stocks of small companies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Equities are generally considered the riskiest class of assets. Dividends aside, they offer no guarantees, and investors’ money is subject to the successes and failures of private businesses in a fiercely competitive marketplace. Equity investing involves buying stock in a private company or group of companies.

Concept note-2: -Bonds or other fixed-income investments – Fixed-income investments are investments in debt securities that pay a rate of return in the form of interest. Such investments are generally considered less risky than investing in equities or other asset classes.
