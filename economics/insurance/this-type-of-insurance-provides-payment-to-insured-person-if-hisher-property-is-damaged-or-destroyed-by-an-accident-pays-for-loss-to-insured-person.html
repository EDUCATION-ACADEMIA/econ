INSURANCE

[SOURCES]
This type of insurance provides payment to insured person if his/her property is damaged or destroyed by an accident; pays for loss to insured person

(A) ** property

(B) liability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Home insurance also known as property insurance or homeowner insurance covers the structure as well as the content. A home insurance policy provides coverage for your own house, rented house, apartment or luxury mansion as well.

Concept note-2: -Different types of general insurance include motor insurance, health insurance, travel insurance, and home insurance.

Concept note-3: -Third-party insurance, which is also sometimes referred to as ‘act-only’ insurance is a statutory requirement for all vehicle owners as per the Motor Vehicle Act. It is a type of insurance cover where the insurer offers protection against damage to the third-party vehicle, personal property and physical injury.

Concept note-4: -Property insurance provides financial reimbursement to the owner or renter of a structure and its contents in case there is damage or theft-and to a person other than the owner or renter if that person is injured on the property.
