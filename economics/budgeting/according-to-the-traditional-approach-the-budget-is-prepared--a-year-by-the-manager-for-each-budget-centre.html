BUDGETING

[SOURCES]
According to the traditional approach, the budget is prepared ____ a year by the manager for each budget centre.

(A) ** once

(B) twice

(C) every months

(D) every quarters

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Traditional budgeting is a method that depends on the same year’s spending to do the budgeting for the current year. The only benefit of going for this sort of budgeting is simplicity. If a company follows this type of budgeting, it doesn’t need to rethink every item on the list.

Concept note-2: -Budget periods are usually 12 months long but may be shorter or longer, if appropriate. Sometimes referred to as the Budget Year.

Concept note-3: -Zero-based budgeting (ZBB) is a method of budgeting in which all expenses must be justified for each new period. The process of zero-based budgeting starts from a “zero base, ” and every function within an organization is analyzed for its needs and costs.

Concept note-4: -ZBB is an operating, planning and budgeting process which requires each manager to justify his entire budget request in detail from scratch (hence : zero-base) and shifts the burden of proof to each manager to justify why he should spend at all.
