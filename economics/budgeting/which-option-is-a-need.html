EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Car options are add-on features beyond the standard equipment built into a new vehicle. They can be pre-installed at the factory before arriving at dealerships, ordered in advance by customers, or installed by the dealer. Car options upgrade or supplement the model’s standard equipment group.

Concept note-2: -You Cannot Rely on Public Transportation Therefore, you cannot simply rely on public transportation for daily commutes. This is why having your own personal vehicle is very important; it helps get you to your destinations, without having to rely on other people.
