Water, Cable, Electricity, Internet and Cell Phone are all examples of what?

(A) optional

(B) ** utilities

(C) wants

(D) luxuries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Common utility services are water, sewer, electric, gas, garbage, cable TV, internet, and mobile services.

Concept note-2: -Utility services include telecommunications, electrical utilities, natural gas, certain transportation services, and also water and wastewater treatment services provided by private companies.

Concept note-3: -Utilities (water, electricity and gas) are essential services that play a vital role in economic and social development.

Concept note-4: -Typically, utilities include electricity, water, and gas bills. You can also add sewage, trash, and recycling, as well as TV, internet, phone, and streaming services to the list. ⏰ TIP: Utility bills are often used as proof of residence, especially for licensing and passport needs.

Concept note-5: -Utilities in a home include electricity, gas, water, sewer, Internet, telephone, cable TV, security systems and, in some areas, trash collection. These essentials are the things you need in daily life to ensure you have a working, comfortable, livable space.
