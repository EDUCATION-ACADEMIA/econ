Which of the countries below uses a Socialist Economy?

(A) USA

(B) Cuba

(C) ** France

(D) Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Communism has been a part of French politics since the early 20th century at the latest. It has been described as “an enduring presence on the French political scene” for most of the 20th century. In 1920, the French Section of the Communist International was founded.

Concept note-2: -The defeat of the Paris Commune (1871) greatly reduced the power and influence of the socialist movements in France. Its leaders were killed or exiled. France’s first socialist party, the Federation of the Socialist Workers of France (FTSF), was founded in 1879.

Concept note-3: -Socialism is an economic system characterised by social ownership and control of the means of production and cooperative management of the economy, and a political philosophy advocating such a system.
