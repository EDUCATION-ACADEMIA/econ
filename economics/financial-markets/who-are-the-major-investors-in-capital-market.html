Who are the major investors in capital market

(A) Individual Investors

(B) ** Insurance companies

(C) Government Agencies

(D) Treasury Department

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the primary market, there are four key players: corporations, institutions, investment banks, and public accounting firms. Institutions invest capital in corporations that seek to expand and grow their businesses, while corporations issue debt or equity to institutions in return for their capital investment.

Concept note-2: -The Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), along with the Reserve Bank of India are the two regulatory authority for Indian securities market, to protect investors and improve the microstructure of capital markets in India.

Concept note-3: -Market actors include individual retail investors, mutual funds, banks, insurance companies, hedge funds, and corporations.
