Which among the statements is NOT TRUE on the concept of relative inflation rate?

(A) Changes in relative inflation rates can affect international trade activity.

(B) Changes in relative inflation rates influences the demand for and supply of currencies

(C) Changes in relative inflation rates can affect exchange rates.

(D) ** Changes in relative inflation rates does not influences to increase the exchange rate of foreign currencies.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When inflation is higher, this tends to have a depressing affect on the value of a country’s currency. This is because increased inflation reduces the currency’s buying power, which weakens it against other currencies. The impact of increasing inflation on currency conversion rates is usually downwards.

Concept note-2: -Exchange rates, which give the price of a country’s currency relative to foreign currencies, fluctuate based on global market dynamics. These fluctuations can affect domestic inflation rates.

Concept note-3: -Higher inflation can also impact exports by having a direct impact on input costs such as materials and labor. These higher costs can have a substantial impact on the competitiveness of exports in the international trade environment.
