EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer: Concept: Equilibrium rate of exchange is determined where demand for foreign exchange is equal to supply of foreign exchange.

Concept note-2: -There are many ways to measure an exchange rate. The most common way is to measure a bilateral exchange rate. A bilateral exchange rate refers to the value of one currency relative to another. Bilateral exchange rates are typically quoted against the US dollar (USD), as it is the most traded currency globally.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Flexible exchange rate. Flexible exchange rate system is a method of regulating exchange rates based on the market mechanism that is demand and supply.
