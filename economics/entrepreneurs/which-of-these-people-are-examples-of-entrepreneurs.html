[SOURCES]
Which of these people are examples of entrepreneurs?

(A) Beyonce and Justin Beiber

(B) ** Steve Jobs and Oprah Winfrey

(C) Angelina Jolie and Bob Barker

(D) James T. Kirk and Mr. Spock

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elon Musk, Steve Jobs, Oprah Winfrey, and Richard Branson. What do these individuals have in common? They are examples of serial entrepreneurs, people who have started multiple businesses.

Concept note-2: -Oprah Winfrey is an American television personality, actress, and entrepreneur whose syndicated daily talk show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, was among the most popular of the genre. She became one of the richest and most influential women in the United States.

Concept note-3: -Andrew Carnegie, Henry Ford, Oprah Winfrey, Bill Gates, and Larry Page are among the most celebrated American entrepreneurs in history.

Concept note-4: -Entertainment Entrepreneur Not content to work for someone else, Oprah founded her own production facility in 1988, Harpo Studios (that’s “Oprah” spelled backward, not a Marx Brothers reference). Since then, it has grown into Harpo, Inc.
