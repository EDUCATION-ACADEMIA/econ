Concept note-2: -That is why the distribution function is so important. This function gets products from producers to consumers so they are on hand when consumers want to buy. Efficient distribution allows businesses to have adequate supplies of products in the right place at the right time.

Concept note-3: -Marketing/Sales The marketing function also includes promoting goods and services, determining how the goods and services will be delivered and developing a pricing strategy to capture market share while remaining competitive.
