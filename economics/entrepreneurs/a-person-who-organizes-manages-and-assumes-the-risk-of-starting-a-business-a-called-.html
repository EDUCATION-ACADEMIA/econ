A person who organizes, manages and assumes the risk of starting a business a called ____

(A) a purchasing agent

(B) a change

(C) a facilitator

(D) ** an entrepreneur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines an entrepreneur as: “one who organizes, manages and assumes the risk of a business or enterprise".

Concept note-2: -An entrepreneur is someone who organizes, manages, and assumes the risks of a business or enterprise.

Concept note-3: -An entrepreneur is someone who has an idea and who works to create a product or service that people will buy, as well as an organization to support that effort. An entrepreneur takes on most of the risk and initiative for their new business, and is often seen as a visionary or innovator.

Concept note-4: -The Merriam-Webster Dictionary defines an entrepreneur as “a person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on greater than normal financial risks in order to do so.” This definition hardly seems to require an advanced degree or some special initiative.
