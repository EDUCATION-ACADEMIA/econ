(A) 1.Entrepreneurs go without food and keep making rash decisions.

(B) 2. Will lead to endless goal searching for entrepreneurs.

(C) ** 3. Will support to aspire for growth so entrepreneurs constantly keep improving themselves.

(D) 4. Remaining foolish refers to them daring to think just different all the time.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"It was their farewell message as they signed off.” He wished that for the graduating students, he added, just as he’d always wished it for himself: Stay hungry. Stay foolish. That’s advice we can all follow.

Concept note-2: -There is always more to do/gain (“stay hungry” for knowledge) and more to learn (“stay foolish”, meaning never consider oneself an expert but a fool in order to learn more).

Concept note-3: -Stay foolish. ‘ Steve Jobs, the late Apple co-founder being called the Thomas Edison of his time, revealed in a commencement speech at Stanford University in 2005 why he dropped out of college-and why he thought it was one of the best things he ever did.
