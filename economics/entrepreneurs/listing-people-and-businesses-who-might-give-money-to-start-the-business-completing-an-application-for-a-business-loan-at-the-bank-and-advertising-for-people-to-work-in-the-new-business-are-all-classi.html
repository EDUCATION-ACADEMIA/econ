(A) Concept Development

(B) ** Resourcing

(C) Actualization

(D) Harvesting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The term business-to-consumer (B2C) refers to the process of selling products and services directly between a business and consumers who are the end-users of its products or services. Most companies that sell directly to consumers can be referred to as B2C companies.

Concept note-2: -What is an example of an activity for a business person who is entering the harvesting stage of entrepreneurship? Emma and Ivy are considering beginning a jewelry-making business. They have been collecting, reviewing, and interpreting the surveys they distributed last week about their business idea.

Concept note-3: -Stage 1: Inspiration and ideation. Stage 2: Execution. Stage 3: Scaling the business. Step 4: Reaching the top of the mountain. 07-Nov-2019
