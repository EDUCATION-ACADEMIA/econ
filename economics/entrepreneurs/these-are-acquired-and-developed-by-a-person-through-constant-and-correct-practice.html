ENTREPRENEURS

[SOURCES]
These are acquired and developed by a person through constant and correct practice.

(A) ** Skills

(B) Practice

(C) Traits

(D) Success

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Skills are acquired and developed by a person through constant and correct practice. They connote dexterity, excellence, and expertise in the performance of particular tasks. Entrepreneurial skills refer to the set of cognitive, technical, and interpersonal skills required in the practice of entrepreneurship.

Concept note-2: -What are acquired skills? Acquired skills are talents and expertise often obtained through education or experience. These include soft skills such as communication and hard or technical skills such as computer programming.

Concept note-3: -cultivate. verb. to develop something such as an attitude, ability, or skill.

Concept note-4: -Some of the examples of skill acquisition are: Anticipation of needs, Collaboration, Customer service, Document management Interpersonal skills, Problem-solving, Resourcefulness, Time management and communication skills are some of the example of skills acquisition in the administrative industry or field.

Concept note-5: -Some personal skills include integrity, communication, work ethic, leadership, problem-solving, confidence and interpersonal skills. Developing these skills can help develop your potential, set meaningful goals and live a more fulfilling life.
