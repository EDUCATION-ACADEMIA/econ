Taylor Swift was the highest earning celebrity of 2019, how much did she earn in that year alone?

(A) $11.6 million

(B) $1.6 million

(C) $187 million

(D) ** $185 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On Wednesday, Forbes released its annual list of the 100 highest-paid celebrities, and the 29-year-old pop star is ranked No. 1 in 2019 with $185 million.

Concept note-2: -And the outlet reported that she made $185 million in 2019 alone (a pay raise of 131 percent from the $80 million she made in 2018).

Concept note-3: -Dwayne Johnson is the highest-paid actor in the world, having earned $89.4 million before taxes last year, Forbes reports in its 2019 list of the highest-paid actors.

Concept note-4: -In 2019, actor Akshay Kumar was the leading celebrity in India with earnings valued at over 2.9 billion Indian rupees.

Concept note-5: -Taylor Swift is the highest-paid female entertainer in the world, earning $92 million in 2022. Taylor Swift attends the 65th Grammy Awards on Feb. 5, 2023 in Los Angeles.
