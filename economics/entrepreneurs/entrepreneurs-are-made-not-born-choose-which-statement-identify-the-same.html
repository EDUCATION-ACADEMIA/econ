“Entrepreneurs are made not born”. Choose which statement identify the same

(A) 1. Entrepreneurs constantly seek work from other job providers

(B) 2. Entrepreneurs usually stumble when the pressures are immense.

(C) ** 3. Entrepreneur rapidly form his or her own opinion and when frustrated; reject the system and form his or her own business or enterprise to achieve objective

(D) 4. Entrepreneur delegates action and supervises people. It takes most of his /her and time to do work.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rather it is achieved through experiences, knowledge and passion. The same goes for business. Entrepreneurs are not marked from birth; they are people who made a series of good decisions in their businesses and lives. Therefore, entrepreneurs are not born; they are made.

Concept note-2: -There are three main types of entrepreneurs: The Creator, The Builder, and The Operator.

Concept note-3: -According to entrepreneurs, three factors are most important for enabling a company’s growth: access to markets, the supply of human capital (i.e. people with the right skills for the job) and access to funding.

Concept note-4: -Discipline. The number one quality of an entrepreneur is self-discipline. Curiosity. The best entrepreneurs always want to learn more. Creativity. Willingness to try things. Honesty. Always having a plan. Understanding the value of self-care. Taking risks. 14-Apr-2022
