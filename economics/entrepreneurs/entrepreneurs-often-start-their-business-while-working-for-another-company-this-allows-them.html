(A) ** to increase their income.

(C) spread themselves too thin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Startup Entrepreneurs Startup entrepreneurs have an idea for a new business and are willing to take the risk of starting one. They are driven by the desire to create something new and potentially make a significant impact in their industry.

Concept note-2: -Freedom and passion, not money, is the main motivation. Cox Business found that more than half of small business owners start their own businesses to be their own boss. They were also motivated by the idea of creating something from the ground up.

Concept note-3: -Many entrepreneurs strive to develop products and services that benefit society as well as creating financial opportunity for the companyâ€™s founders. Some go so far as to make personal financial goals secondary to contributing to society.

Concept note-4: -The serial entrepreneur The serial type of entrepreneurship is someone who starts and leads one business after another. Although, it is also used for the small business entrepreneur who runs multiple businesses at the same time. By definition, the serial type has an entrepreneurial personality.
