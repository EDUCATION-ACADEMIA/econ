ENTREPRENEURS

[SOURCES]
Entrepreneurs are dreamers and believe their ideas are possible, even when they seem unattainable.

(A) Hardworking

(B) Opportunity Seeker

(C) ** Optimistic

(D) Self Confidence

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What’s the difference between an entrepreneur and a dreamer? An entrepreneur has a dream and seeks out mentors that have a successful history who are willing to guide and teach you. Dreamers have a great idea but often take uncalculated risks and rely on luck.

Concept note-2: -Entrepreneurs have a particular spirit about them, a mindset that keeps them going through the challenges and the victories. At heart, they are optimistic about their business and their future. It is fascinating to discover how optimism helps entrepreneurs succeed.

Concept note-3: -Optimism: It’s difficult to succeed at anything if you don’t believe in a good outcome. Entrepreneurs are dreamers and believe their ideas are possible, even when they seem unattainable.

Concept note-4: -When it comes to business, dreaming is the biggest motivator an entrepreneur can cultivate-dreaming of a better world, for themselves, their company, their community. It’s one of the greatest sources of motivation for any human being, but especially entrepreneurs.
