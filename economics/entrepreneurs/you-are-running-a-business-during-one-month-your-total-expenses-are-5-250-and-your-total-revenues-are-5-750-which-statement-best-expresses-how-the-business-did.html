You are running a business. During one month your total expenses are $5, 250 and your total revenues are $5, 750. Which statement best expresses how the business did?

(A) ** It was in the black

(B) It was in the red

(C) It was neutral

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Total Expenses = Net Revenue-Net Income.

Concept note-2: -Total costs = fixed costs + variable costs Question Calculate: The total fixed costs incurred by the sandwich shop. The total variable costs incurred by the sandwich shop.

Concept note-3: -Key Takeaways In accounting, the breakeven point is calculated by dividing the fixed costs of production by the price per unit minus the variable costs of production. The breakeven point is the level of production at which the costs of production equal the revenues for a product.

Concept note-4: -Revenue – Cost of Goods Sold – Expenses = Net Income. Gross Income – Expenses = Net Income. Total Revenues – Total Expenses = Net Income. Gross income = $60, 000-$20, 000 = $40, 000. Expenses = $6, 000 + $2, 000 + $10, 000 + $1, 000 + $1, 000 = $20, 000. Net income = $40, 000-$20, 000 = $20, 000. More items •08-Oct-2021
