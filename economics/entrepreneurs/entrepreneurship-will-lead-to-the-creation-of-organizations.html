(A) ** TRUE

(B) FALSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -True: Entrepreneurship will lead to the creation of organizations. True: Entrepreneurship can improve the life of the entrepreneur only.

Concept note-2: -Entrepreneurship will lead to the creation of organizations. 5. Entrepreneurship can improve the life of the entrepreneur only.

Concept note-4: -Entrepreneurship Promotes Innovation Through the right practices of research and development, entrepreneurs bring new innovation that opens the door of new ventures, markets, products, and technology.
