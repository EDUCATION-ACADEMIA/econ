Which of the following is a FALSE statement about sales promotion activities?

(A) ** Sales promotion activities focus on generating long-term sales.

(B) Results are measurable, particularly with contests, coupons, and sweepstakes.

(C) The disadvantage of sales promotion include too much focus on the short-term and losses in profits.

(D) Sales promotion activities include things such as coupons, contests, rebates, & sweepstakes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The incorrect option is Sales-promotion is recurring and Multiple time communication process. Sales Promotion: Sales promotion is one of the elements of promotional mix. To increase consumer demand, sales promotion includes both media and non-media marketing communication.

Concept note-2: -Online marketing does not qualify as a sales promotion as it does not offer any incentive to the consumer on the purchase of the company’s product. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Examples include contests, coupons, freebies, loss leaders, point of purchase displays, premiums, prizes, product samples, and rebates. Sales promotions can be directed at either the customer, sales staff, or distribution channel members (such as retailers).

Concept note-4: -Sales promotions are used to stimulate purchasing and sales and the objectives are to increase sales, inform potential customers about new products, and create a positive business or corporate image. Examples: coupons, product samples, point-of-purchase display.

Concept note-5: -Product discounts. Offering discounts on products is one of the most popular types of sales promotions. Loyalty programs. Loyal customers can contribute heavily to a company’s success, and setting up a loyalty program is a great strategy for retaining customers. Holiday promotions.
