FEDERAL RESERVE

DOWNLOAD FREE PDF BOOK (USEFUL FOR TEACHERS/INSTRUCTORS/COACHING INSTITUTES)

Federal Reserve Bank employees are considered to be government employees.

(A) true

(B) ** false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Federal Reserve Banks are not a part of the federal government, but they exist because of an act of Congress. Their purpose is to serve the public. So is the Fed private or public? The answer is both.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India was established following the Reserve Bank of India Act of 1934. Though privately owned initially, it was nationalised in 1949 and since then fully owned by the Ministry of Finance, Government of India (GoI).

Concept note-3: -Which of the following is true of the Federal Reserve System? It is the central bank of the United States, It is responsible for setting monetary policy, It is responsible for regulating the banking system. (All of the choices are correct.)

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is B. Monetary policy is established by the Federal Open Market Committee. The Federal Reserve System has twelve Federal Reserve banks whereby there is one represented in each Federal Reserve districts.
