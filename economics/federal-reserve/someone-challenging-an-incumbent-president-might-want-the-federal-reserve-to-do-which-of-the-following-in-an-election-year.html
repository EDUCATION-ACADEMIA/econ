Someone challenging an incumbent president might want the Federal Reserve to do which of the following in an election year?

(A) ** Increase interest rates

(B) Decrease interest rates

(C) Engage in quantitative easing

(D) Recommend to Congress that fiscal stimulus be increased

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On a four-quarter change basis, total PCE price inflation was expected to be 5.5 percent in 2022, while core inflation was expected to be 4.7 percent, both lower than in the November projection.

Concept note-2: -The president is responsible for all of the Reserve Bank’s activities, including monetary policy, bank supervision and regulation, and payments services. In addition, the president serves on the Federal Reserve’s chief monetary policymaking body, the Federal Open Market Committee (FOMC).

Concept note-3: -The Federal Reserve seeks to control inflation by influencing interest rates. When inflation is too high, the Federal Reserve typically raises interest rates to slow the economy and bring inflation down.

Concept note-4: -Manipulating Interest Rates. Open Market Operations. Reserve Requirements. Influencing Market Perceptions. Securities Lending Facility. Quantitative Easing. The Bottom Line.
