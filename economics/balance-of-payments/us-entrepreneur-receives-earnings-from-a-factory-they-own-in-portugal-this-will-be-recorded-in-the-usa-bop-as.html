US entrepreneur receives earnings from a factory they own in Portugal. This will be recorded in the USA BoP as,

(A) Invisible balance inflow

(B) ** Investment income inflow

(C) Financial ac inflow

(D) Transfers inflow

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The balance of payments is a system of recording transactions that happen between countries. Any movement of money into, or out of, a country has to be accounted for.

Concept note-2: -Capital account – The record of a country’s international transactions involving purchases or sales of financial and real assets (borrowing, lending, and investments).

Concept note-3: -The balance of payments (BOP) is the record of all international financial transactions made by the residents of a country. There are three main categories of the BOP: the current account, the capital account, and the financial account.

Concept note-4: -Foreign direct investment (FDI) is an ownership stake in a foreign company or project made by an investor, company, or government from another country.
