An economy has a high rate of inflation and a balance of payments deficit.Which policy change would help to reduce the balance of payments deficit without makinginflation worse?(Winter 2014)

(A) A a devaluation of the currency

(B) ** B a reduction in government spending

(C) C the imposition of import quotas

(D) D the removal of import tariffs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In general, the federal government has two types of tools available to fight inflation. Monetary policy, conducted by the Federal Reserve, can raise interest rates. Or fiscal policy, controlled by the Congress and President, can adjust taxes and spending.

Concept note-2: -Using fiscal policy to promote economic growth to increase tax revenue and decrease spending can decrease a deficit.

Concept note-3: -Quantitative easing, cutting tax rates, lowering interest rates, open market operations, lowering bank reserve limits, increasing spending by the Government are the ways in which deflation can be controlled.

Concept note-4: -This is called expansionary monetary policy. 8. During times of inflation, by raising taxes or decreasing government spending, fiscal policy attempts to decrease consumer spending and business investment, which should lead to a decrease in aggregate demand and a decrease in real GDP, the price level, and employment.
