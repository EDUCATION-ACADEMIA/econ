Which of the following is NOT one of the characteristics of money?

(A) durable

(B) stable

(C) scarce

(D) ** affordable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer is Option C) The supply of Money remains fixed. Good Money has many characteristics like Stability, homogeneity, profitability, divisibility, durability, etc. The supply of Money is not included in the characteristics of Money.

Concept note-2: -in fact, a variety of items have been used as money over the centuries. The items that worked the best tended to have four basic characteristics: portability, divisibility, durability, and acceptability.

Concept note-3: -The characteristics of money are durability, portability, divisibility, uniformity, limited supply, and acceptability.
