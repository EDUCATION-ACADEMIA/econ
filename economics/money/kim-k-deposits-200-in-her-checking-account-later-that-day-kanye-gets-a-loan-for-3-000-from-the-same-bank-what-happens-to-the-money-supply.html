Kim K. deposits $200 in her checking account. Later that day Kanye gets a loan for $3, 000 from the same bank. What happens to the money supply

(A) increases by $200.

(B) increases by $2800.

(C) decreases by $3000.

(D) ** increases by $3000.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Whenever a bank grants a loan, it creates a deposit or a liability against itself. 2. Deposits of the bank circulate as money, the creation of such deposits lead to a net increase in the money stock.

Concept note-2: -$90, 000 in checkable deposit liabilities and $32, 000 in reserves. the receipts became in effect paper money. When a bank accepts a checkable deposit from a customer, its deposits will increase and its excess reserves will increase by the same amount as deposits.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, to get the maximum amount of loans that can be issued, you multiply the size of the deposit by 1 minus the reserve ratio.

Concept note-4: -The maximum amount by which banks can increase deposits in the entire banking system is $5 billion.
