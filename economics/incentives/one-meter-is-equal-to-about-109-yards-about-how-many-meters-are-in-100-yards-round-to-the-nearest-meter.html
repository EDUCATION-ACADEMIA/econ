One meter is equal to about 1.09 yards. About how many meters are in 100 yards? Round to the nearest meter.

(A) 91.7 meters

(B) 9.17 meters

(C) ** 92 meters

(D) 920 meters

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -100 yards is equal to 91.44 meters.

Concept note-2: -1 meter is equivalent to 1.0936 yards, 39.370 inches, and 0.0254 meters.

Concept note-3: -The 100-yard dash is a track and field sprint event of 100 yards (91.44 metres). It was part of the Commonwealth Games until 1970, and was included in the triathlon of the Olympics in 1904. It is not generally used in international events, replaced by the 100-metre sprint (109.36 yards).

Concept note-4: -The difference between meter and yard is that the meter is a SI unit of length and a yard is a unit of length. Also, 1 meter is about 1.09 yards.

Concept note-5: -109.4 Yards. Generally 110 Yards is run in comparison to 100 Meter.
