Paul’s aunt gives him a birthday gift of $700 in cash. Paul deposits the money into an account that earns simple interest at a rate of 4%. Paul makes no withdrawals. How much interest has his account earned after 7 years?

(A) $4, 900

(B) $19, 600

(C) ** $196.00

(D) $7.00

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What rate of simple interest is needed for $700 to double, in 3 years? The interest rate required is 33.3% Page 3 8.

Concept note-2: -Installments Under Simple Interest This will be equal to the total interest charged for n months i.e. [P+ (P* n* r)/ 12* 100].

Concept note-3: -Summary: An investment of $10000 today invested at 6% for five years at simple interest will be $13, 000.

Concept note-4: -For example, the interest on a $30, 000, 36-month loan at 6% is $2, 856. The same loan ($30, 000 at 6%) paid back over 72 months would cost $5, 797 in interest.
