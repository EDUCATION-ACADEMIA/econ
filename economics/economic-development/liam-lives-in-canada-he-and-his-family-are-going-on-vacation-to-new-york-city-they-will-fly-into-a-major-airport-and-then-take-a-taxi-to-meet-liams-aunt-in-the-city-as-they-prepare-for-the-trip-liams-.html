(A) requesting a passport from Canada

(B) applying for citizenship in the United States

(C) withdrawing a large amount of Canadian money

(D) ** exchanging Canadian money for US money

As with other developed nations, the country’s economy is dominated by the service industry which employs about three quarters of Canadians.
