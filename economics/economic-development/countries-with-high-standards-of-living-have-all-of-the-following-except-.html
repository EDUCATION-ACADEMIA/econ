Countries with high standards of living have all of the following EXCEPT ____

(A) Low infant mortality rates

(B) High literacy rates

(C) ** Short life expectancy

(D) Long life expectancy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The definition of standard of living is the level of material wealth and income available to a person or community. It is usually measured by standards such as life expectancy, literacy rates, access to education and health care, and housing conditions.

Concept note-2: -Developing countries are countries that have a low standard living; these countries usually have a low gross national income per capita even though they are in an economical development. They also have a high gross domestic product per capita. Another economic measure is also industrialization.
