Which of these is neither a good nor a service?

(A) A gallon of gasoline

(B) A piano lesson

(C) A meal at a restaurant

(D) ** A $20 bill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Demand refers to the consumerâ€™s desire and willingness to buy a product or service at a given period or over time.

Concept note-2: -Economists use the term demand to refer to the amount of some good or service consumers are willing and able to purchase at each price.

Concept note-3: -Demand is simply the quantity of a good or service that consumers are willing and able to buy at a given price in a given time period.

Concept note-4: -Lesson 2: Law of Supply The quantity supplied of any good or service is the amount of a good that sellers are willing and able to sell at a particular price. Many factors affect supply, but only price can determine the quantity supplied.
