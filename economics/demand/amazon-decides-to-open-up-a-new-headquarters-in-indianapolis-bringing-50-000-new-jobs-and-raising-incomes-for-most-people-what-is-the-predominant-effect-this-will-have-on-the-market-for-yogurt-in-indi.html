DEMAND

[SOURCES]
Amazon decides to open up a new headquarters in Indianapolis, bringing 50, 000 new jobs and raising incomes for most people. What is the predominant effect this will have on the market for yogurt in Indianapolis?

(A) ** Demand will increase

(B) Demand will decrease

(C) Supply will increase

(D) Supply will decrease

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An improvement in technology that reduces the cost of production will cause an increase in supply. Alternatively, you can think of this as a reduction in price necessary for firms to supply any quantity. Either way, this can be shown as a rightward (or downward) shift in the supply curve.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer to the given question is option D. the law of demand. As per the law of demand, the price of a good or service usually has an inverse relationship with the quantity demanded. Thus, consumers tend to buy less of a good or service when its price increases and vice-versa.

Concept note-3: -The supply a good decreases if the price of one of its complements in production falls. Resource and input prices influence the cost of production. And the more it costs to produce a good, the smaller is the quantity supplied of that good. Expectations about future prices influence supply.

Concept note-4: -The demand for a good increases or decreases depending on several factors. This includes the productâ€™s price, perceived quality, advertising spend, consumer income, consumer confidence, and changes in taste and fashion.
