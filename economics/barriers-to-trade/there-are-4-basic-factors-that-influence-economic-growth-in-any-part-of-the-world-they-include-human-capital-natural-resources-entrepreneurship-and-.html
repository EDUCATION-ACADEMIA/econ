There are 4 basic factors that influence economic growth in any part of the world. They include human capital, natural resources, entrepreneurship and ____

(A) courses

(B) ** capital goods

(C) conclusion

(D) cancer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Economists divide the factors of production into four categories: land, labor, capital, and entrepreneurship. The first factor of production is land, but this includes any natural resource used to produce goods and services. This includes not just land, but anything that comes from the land.

Concept note-2: -Economists define four factors of production: land, labor, capital and entrepreneurship. These can be considered the building blocks of an economy. How these factors are combined determines the success or failure of the outcome.

Concept note-3: -Factors of production are essential to any economy, as they are the building blocks for all goods and services.

Concept note-4: -Factors of production is an economic term that describes the inputs used in the production of goods or services to make an economic profit. These include any resource needed for the creation of a good or service. The factors of production are land, labor, capital, and entrepreneurship.
