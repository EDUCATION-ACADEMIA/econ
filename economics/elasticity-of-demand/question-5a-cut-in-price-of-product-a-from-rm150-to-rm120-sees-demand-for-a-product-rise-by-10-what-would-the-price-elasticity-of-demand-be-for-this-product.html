Question 5A cut in price of Product A from RM1.50 to RM1.20 sees demand for a product rise by 10%. What would the price elasticity of demand be for this product?

(A) 3.00

(B) 1.00

(C) 2.00

(D) ** -0.50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thus, the answer is (d). A 10 percent increase in the price will cause a 5 percent decrease in the quantity demanded.

Concept note-2: -If the elasticity of supply is 0.5, then a 10% decrease in price will result in a 5% increase in quantity supplied.

Concept note-3: -The price elasticity of demand is calculated as the percentage change in quantity divided by the percentage change in price.

Concept note-4: -Inelastic demand occurs when changes in price cause a disproportionately small change in quantity demanded. For example, a good with inelastic demand might see its price increase by 30%, but demand falls by only 10% as a result.
