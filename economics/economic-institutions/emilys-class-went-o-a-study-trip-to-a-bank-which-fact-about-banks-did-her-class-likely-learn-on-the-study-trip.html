Emily’s class went o a study trip to a bank. Which fact about bank’s did her class likely learn on the study trip?

(A) Bank borrow money from people. Bank do NOT pay them interest.

(B) Banks give money to people. People do NOT have to pay the money back.

(C) ** Bank lend money to people. People pay the money back with interest.

(D) Banks make money. Bank print their own bills and create their own money

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 4 different types of banks are Central Bank, Commercial Bank, Cooperative Banks, Regional Rural Banks.

Concept note-2: -The 5 most important banking services are checking and savings accounts, loan and mortgage services, wealth management, providing Credit and Debit Cards, Overdraft services.

Concept note-3: -A bank which is entrusted with the functions of guiding and regulating the banking system of a country is known as its Central bank. Such a bank does not deal with the general public. It acts essentially as Government’s banker, maintain deposit accounts of all other banks and advances money to other banks when needed.

Concept note-4: -Based on the exchange of cash, there are three types of accounting transactions, namely cash transactions, non-cash transactions, and credit transactions.
