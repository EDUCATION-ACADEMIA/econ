In the UK, investment accounts for ____ of GDP

(A) ** 15%

(B) 29%

(C) 10%

(D) 72%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -United Kingdom Investment: % of GDP United Kingdom Investment accounted for 16.8 % of its Nominal GDP in Sep 2022, compared with a ratio of 18.3 % in the previous quarter. UK investment share of Nominal GDP data is updated quarterly, available from Mar 1955 to Sep 2022, with an average ratio of 18.8 %.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, agriculture contributed around 0.68 percent to the United Kingdom’s GDP, 17.49 percent came from the manufacturing industry, and 71.46 percent from the services sector. The vast majority of the UK’s GDP is generated by the services sector, and tourism in particular keeps the economy going.
