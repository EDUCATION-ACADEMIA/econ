Your teacher is providing a

(A) Good

(B) Commercial

(C) ** Service

(D) Money

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Teaching is the specialized application of knowledge, skills and attributes designed to provide unique service to meet the educational needs of the individual and of society. The choice of learning activities whereby the goals of education are realized in the school is the responsibility of the teaching profession.

Concept note-2: -Examples of people who provide a service are teachers, doctors, and mechanics. Some people provide a service in which they create goods.

Concept note-3: -Related Definitions Classroom teaching service means employment, as a classroom teacher holding a residency or professional cer-tificate, in an approved education program on at least a half-time basis. This also includes comparable employment as a substitute teacher or in part-time teaching positions.

Concept note-4: -Some qualities of a good teacher include skills in communication, listening, collaboration, adaptability, empathy and patience. Other characteristics of effective teaching include an engaging classroom presence, value in real-world learning, exchange of best practices and a lifelong love of learning.
