Concept note-1: -A baker is a producer who cooks up yummy cakes, and a painter is a producer who creates artwork for us to admire. Doctors, hair stylists, landscapers, and mechanics are producers. When you draw a picture, or when you make cookies, you are a producer. People are consumers, too.

Concept note-2: -The producer is an artist who can lead a project all the way through the process from idea to audience on his own. Simply put, the producer is a man who makes sure that his own or someone elseâ€™s creativity is realized; materialized.

Concept note-3: -Recruit and contract a company of artists for each production. Monitor the quality and progress of the show in rehearsal, in liaison with the creative team, and later on tour. Book tours into venues and manage contracts and co-production agreements. Oversee the marketing, print and press campaign for each production.

Concept note-4: -April 2021) An artist is a person engaged in an activity related to creating art, practicing the arts, or demonstrating an art. The common usage in both everyday speech and academic discourse refers to a practitioner in the visual arts only.
