Which statement is an example of someone being a producer?

(A) ** Cook bakinng chicken dishes for his customers to eat

(B) Cook eating cookies

(C) Cook smelling herbs

(D) Cook wrapping cookies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the Cambridge dictionary, a cook is ‘someone who prepares and cooks food’, while a chef is ‘a skilled and trained cook who works in a hotel or restaurant’.

Concept note-2: -Examples: Rice, pulses, boiled eggs, etc. Some items like vegetables, chicken, and eggs can also be boiled and consumed.

Concept note-3: -A cook is a professional individual who prepares items for consumption in the food industry, especially in settings such as restaurants. A cook is sometimes referred to as a chef, although in the culinary world, the terms are not interchangeable.
