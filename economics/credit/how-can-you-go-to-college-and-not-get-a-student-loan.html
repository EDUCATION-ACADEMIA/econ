How can you go to college and not get a student loan?

(A) Scholarships

(B) Grants

(C) ** Scholarships and Grants and go to a Work Study Program

(D) Get good grades

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, of course. You are allowed to apply to any scholarship for which you qualify independently of any loans.

Concept note-2: -NTSE Scholarship. KVPY-Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana. Swami Vivekananda Merit cum Means Scholarship. UGC Merit Scholarship-Post Graduate Merit Scholarship for University Rank Holders PG Scholarships for Professional Courses.

Concept note-3: -Scholarships. Grants. Work-study. Part-time work. Savings. Income share agreements. Employer sponsorships. Payment plan. More items •28-Dec-2022

Concept note-4: -Know where to look. Prepare in advance. Work hard and keep motivated. Make yourself stand out from other applicants. Read the application instructions carefully. Submit an exceptional scholarship essay or cover letter. Be realistic.
