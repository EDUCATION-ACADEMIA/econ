Kasey is looking at possible options to pay for higher education. What type of credit is best suited for financing this program?

(A) Payday loan

(B) conventional loan

(C) ** student loan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Revolving credit is a credit line that remains available even as you pay the balance. Borrowers can access credit up to a certain amount and then have ongoing access to that amount of credit. They can repay the balance in full, or make regular payments.

Concept note-2: -The three main types of credit are revolving credit, installment, and open credit. Credit enables people to purchase goods or services using borrowed money.

Concept note-3: -Credit cards typically carry higher interest rates than student loans, and can often exceed 20%. Federal student loan interest usually falls below 10%.

Concept note-4: -Revolving Credit. This form of credit allows you to borrow money up to a certain amount. Charge Cards. This form of credit is often mistaken to be the same as a revolving credit card. Installment Credit. Non-Installment or Service Credit. 21-Feb-2014
