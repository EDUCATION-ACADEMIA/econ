COMPOUND INTEREST

[SOURCES]
Diego deposited $2, 000 into a savings account. How much will Diego have in his account after 7 months with 3¼% simple interest?

(A) $2455

(B) $455

(C) $37.92

(D) ** $2037.92

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simple interest on Rs. 2000 for 4 years is Rs. 400.

Concept note-2: -Simple interest works in your favor when you’re a borrower because it keeps the overall amount that you pay lower than it would be with compound interest; however, it can work against you when you’re an investor because you’ll want your returns to compound as much as possible to get the most from your investment.
