Mariam deposited $6000. Find the simple interest at 5.37% for 10 years.

(A) $26.10

(B) $8610

(C) $2610

(D) ** $3222

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Simple interest is calculated with the following formula: S.I. = P × R × T, where P = Principal, R = Rate of Interest in % per annum, and T = Time, usually calculated as the number of years. The rate of interest is in percentage r% and is to be written as r/100.

Concept note-2: -Hence the simple interest is ₹350.

Concept note-3: -Hence, the simple interest on Rs 6000 for 1 year at 4% per annum is Rs 240.

Concept note-4: -The time taken to get a simple interest of rs 1800 on Rs 6000 10% rate is 3 years.
