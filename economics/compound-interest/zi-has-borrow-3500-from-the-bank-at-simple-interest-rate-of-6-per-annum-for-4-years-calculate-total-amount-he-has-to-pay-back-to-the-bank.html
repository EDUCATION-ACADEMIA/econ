COMPOUND INTEREST

[SOURCES]
Zi Has borrow $3500 from the bank at simple interest rate of 6% per annum for 4 years. Calculate total amount he has to pay back to the bank.

(A) ** $4340

(B) $2160

(C) $5600

(D) $840

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you have a 6 percent interest rate and you make monthly payments, you would divide 0.06 by 12 to get 0.005. Multiply that number by your remaining loan balance to find out how much you’ll pay in interest that month. If you have a $5, 000 loan balance, your first month of interest would be $25.

Concept note-2: -interest = RS. 490. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-3: -Principal = $ 3500, Interest = $ 700, Rate = 4% p.a. Therefore, Time (T) = 5 years.

Concept note-4: -For this reason, lenders often like to present interest rates compounded monthly instead of annually. For example, a 6% mortgage interest rate amounts to a monthly 0.5% interest rate. However, after compounding monthly, interest totals 6.17% compounded annually.
