Dale and Lynn purchased their home in 1989 for $89, 000. The house was appraised in 2008 for $325, 000. What was the annual rate of appreciation?

(A) 6.26%

(B) 7.05%

(C) 6.87%

(D) ** 6.69%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -∴ Compound interest is ₹ 1698.58.

Concept note-2: -∴ Amount will be Rs. 3025 and Interest will be Rs. 525 If Compounded Annually.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: The calculated future value of the investment in 2 years is $121.

Concept note-4: -Compound interest will be calculated by C.I = [P × (1+R100)n]-P. C.I = [15000 × (1+5100)5]-15000. C.I = [15000 × (1+120)5]-15000.
