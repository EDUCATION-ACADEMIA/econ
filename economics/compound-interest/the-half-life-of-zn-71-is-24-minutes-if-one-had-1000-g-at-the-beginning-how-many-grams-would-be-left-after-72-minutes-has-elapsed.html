The half-life of Zn-71 is 2.4 minutes. If one had 100.0 g at the beginning, how many grams would be left after 7.2 minutes has elapsed?

(A) 100.0g

(B) 50.0g

(C) ** 12.5g

(D) 8.5g

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Number of half-lives n = 7.2/2.4 = 3 Thus, three half-lives have passed. Amount remaining (N) =? N = N = 100 / 23 N = 100 / 8 N = 12.5 g As a result, the amount of Zn-71 remaining after 7.2 minutes is 12.5 g.

Concept note-2: -The half-life of Zn-71 is 2.4 minutes.
