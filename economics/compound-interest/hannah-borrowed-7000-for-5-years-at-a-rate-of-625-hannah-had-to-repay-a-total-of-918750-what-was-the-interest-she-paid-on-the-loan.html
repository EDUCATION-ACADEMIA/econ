Hannah borrowed $7000 for 5 years at a rate of 6.25%. Hannah had to repay a total of $9187.50. What was the interest she paid on the loan?

(A) ** $2187.50

(B) $7000

(C) 6.25%

(D) $9187.50

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5% = 0.05 . Then multiply the original amount by the interest rate. $1, 000 * 0.05 = $50 . That’s it.

Concept note-2: -Here’s the simple interest formula: Interest = P x R x T. P = Principal amount (the beginning balance). R = Interest rate (usually per year, expressed as a decimal). T = Number of time periods (generally one-year time periods).

Concept note-3: -The simple interest of a loan for $1, 000 with 5 percent interest after 3 years is $ 150.

Concept note-4: -You can calculate your total interest by using this formula: Principal loan amount x interest rate x loan term = interest.
