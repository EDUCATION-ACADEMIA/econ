A sum of money invested at 8% per annum amounts to Rs 12122 in 2 years. What will it amount to in 2 years 8 months at 9% per annum?

(A) ** 12958

(B) 12959

(C) 12957

(D) 12956

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Here, simple interest rate per annum is 8%, the time is 2 years and the final amount is Rs. 12122. Thus, the initial principal balance in hand is Rs. 10, 450.

Concept note-2: -A sum of money invested at 8% per annum amounts to Rs. 12, 122 in years.

Concept note-3: -Rate=8% p.a. Solution: The required sum is Rs. 1100.
