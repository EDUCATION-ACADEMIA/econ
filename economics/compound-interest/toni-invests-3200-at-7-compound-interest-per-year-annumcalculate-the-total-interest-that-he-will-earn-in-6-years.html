Toni invests $3200 at 7% compound interest per year annum.Calculate the total interest that he will earn in 6 years.

(A) $4802.33

(B) $3200

(C) $53

(D) ** $1602.33

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Compound Interest in Rs. 3200 for 1 year at the rate of 8% p.a. (Compounded half-yearly) is 261.12 .

Concept note-2: -30, 000 at 7% per annum is Rs. 4, 347.

Concept note-3: -(30000 + 4347) = Rs. 34347. Let the time be n years. n = 2 years.

Concept note-4: -For example, the interest on a $30, 000, 36-month loan at 6% is $2, 856.
