You invest $55 at 10% compounded annually for 3 years. How much will your investment be worth in 3 years?

(A) $7, 321

(B) $36.79

(C) $18.21

(D) ** $73.21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -compounded, annually at the rate of 10% p.a. for 3 years is Rs 331. Q.

Concept note-2: -Period (n) = 3 years∴ Amount = P (1+R100)n=Rs. 10000 (1+10100)3=Rs. 10000×1110×1110×1110=Rs. 13310C.I.

Concept note-3: -It is calculated by multiplying the first principal amount by one and adding the annual interest rate raised to the number of compound periods subtract one. The total initial amount of your loan is then subtracted from the resulting value. P is principal, I is the interest rate, n is the number of compounding periods.

Concept note-4: -Therefore, Compound interest on the given sum will be Rs. 3972.
