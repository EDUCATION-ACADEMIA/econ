The GDP of the United States is the ____ in the entire world at >$21 trillion.

(A) ** highest

(B) lowest

(C) dumbest

(D) best smelling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Top 5 economy in the world According to the Top 5 economies in the world, the GDP of the USA is 26 trillion dollars.

Concept note-2: -The United States is a highly developed mixed-market economy. It is the world’s largest economy by nominal GDP, and the second-largest by purchasing power parity (PPP) behind China. It has the world’s seventh-highest per capita GDP (nominal) and the eighth-highest per capita GDP (PPP) as of 2022.

Concept note-3: -In 2021, the United States accounted for 15.69 percent of global gross domestic product (GDP) after adjusting for purchasing power parity (PPP). This share was expected to decrease to 14.53 percent by 2027, which is roughly a seventh of the global total. What is PPP?

Concept note-4: -The United States has been the world’s largest economy since 1871. The nominal GDP for the United States is $21.44 trillion. The U.S. GDP (PPP) is also $21.44 trillion.
