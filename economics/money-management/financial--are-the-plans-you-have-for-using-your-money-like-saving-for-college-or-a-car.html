Financial ____ are the plans you have for using your money. (LIke saving for college or a car)

(A) needs

(B) ** goals

(C) wants

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A financial plan paints a comprehensive picture of your current finances, your financial goals and any strategies youâ€™ve set to achieve those goals. Good financial planning should include details about your cash flow, savings, debt, investments, insurance and any other elements of your financial life.

Concept note-2: -Having a written financial plan gives you a measurable goal to work toward. Because you can track your progress, you can reduce doubt or uncertainty about your decisions and make adjustments to help overcome obstacles that could derail you.

Concept note-3: -A financial goal is a target to aim for when managing your money. It can involve saving, spending, earning, or even investing.

Concept note-4: -Solid financial goals for students include creating a budget, opening a savings account, beginning to invest for retirement, establishing an emergency fund, applying for financial aid, beginning to build credit, and using debt as little as possible.
