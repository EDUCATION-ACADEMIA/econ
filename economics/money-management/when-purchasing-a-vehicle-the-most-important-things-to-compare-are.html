When purchasing a vehicle, the most important things to compare are

(A) colors & cost

(B) safety & color

(C) color

(D) ** safety & costs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dual front airbags. ABS with EBD. Reverse parking sensors. Reverse parking camera. Seatbelt pre-tensioner. Cornering stability control. Speed-sensing door lock. Impact sensing door unlock. More items •10-Mar-2022

Concept note-2: -#1 – Airbags. Driver and passenger airbags typically will deploy if you get into an accident that involves the front of your vehicle.

Concept note-3: -Things like forward collision warnings, autobrake, lane-departure warnings, lane-keeping assist, blind-spot detection and park-assist systems all can help reduce your chances of being involved in an accident.

Concept note-4: -Engine. It makes sense to start with the most important part under the hood of a vehicle, which is the engine. Most modern vehicles run on internal combustion engines, which generate energy by igniting a mixture of air and fuel that moves pistons, which in turn move the car.

Concept note-5: -However, automobile accidents have still been the leading cause of injuries and death in most countries. Car safety also pertains to necessary car safety features. This is incorporated in your car to protect you and other passengers from injury and death during a collision or crash.
