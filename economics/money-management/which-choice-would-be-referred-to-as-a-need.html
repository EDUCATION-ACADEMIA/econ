Which choice would be referred to as a “need”?

(A) cell phone

(B) ** food

(C) new tennis shoes

(D) baseball glove

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The food we put into our bodies is our fuel. It provides us with nutrients-the vitamins, minerals, and other compounds our bodies need to function and thrive. Research shows that good food choices are especially important for children’s growing bodies and minds.

Concept note-2: -Food choice refers to how people decide on what to buy and eat. A complex set of factors that vary from person to person and depend on culture, heritage and up-bringing all influence food choice. We may look for price if we need to stick to a budget, or look for allergen information if we have a food allergy.
