Why should you price shop?

(A) To pay more money.

(B) To build interest.

(C) To spend more.

(D) ** To save and get the best deal.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Benefits of Discount Pricing By offering a reduced price for a quality product, less activation energy is needed to inspire customers to make a purchase. For customers who are interested but unsure about buying a product, offering a discount can be a helpful way to close a sale.

Concept note-2: -In simple terms, a special pricing agreement is: An agreement offered jointly between a manufacturer and a distributor to supply products to a defined market segment at a specially reduced price. The prices offered might even be loss making for the distributor without the support that they claim from the manufacturer.

Concept note-3: -Set a budget and make a shopping list. Shop at the right time. Apply coupons. Compare (and match) prices. Use shopping tools and apps. Buy online, pick up in store. Get rewards. Score free stuff. 17-Aug-2021
