Depositing your annual income tax return into an account until you have enough to buy a car

(A) ** Saving

(B) Investing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -disposable-after taxes. discretionary-after taxes and after every day living expenses. the amount of money you deposit into a savings account is called the. principal.

Concept note-2: -If your total interest income is below Rs 10, 000 then you do not have to pay tax on it. However, this does not mean that you can avail of tax free interest income by having multiple savings accounts where the interest earned is below this threshold.

Concept note-3: -Who is eligible for income tax return? While any one having a valid PAN is eligible to file IT return even if their income is below exemption limit, it is mandatory for companies and firms to file ITR. Individuals whose taxable income exceeds the maximum amount not chargeable to tax are eligible for income tax return.
