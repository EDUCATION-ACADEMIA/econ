If ABM firm sells its output in a market, with many sellers and buyers of homogeneous product, and unlimited resource mobility, the structures is a/an

(A) monopolist

(B) oligopolist

(C) ** perfect competitor

(D) monopolistic competitor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Differentiated products and a large number of buyers and sellers characterize monopolistic competition.

Concept note-2: -Perfect competition is a type of market where there are large number of buyers and sellers who deals in homogeneous product due to which no individual unit is able to influence the price of the product and the firms have to quote the price that prevails in the market because of the customer’s knowledge about the price.

Concept note-3: -Perfect Competition They sell similar products (homogeneous), lack price influence over the commodities, and are free to enter or exit the market. Consumers in this type of market have full knowledge of the goods being sold.

Concept note-4: -Perfect competition is a form of the market in which there is a large number of buyers and sellers and where homogeneous product is sold at a uniform price.
