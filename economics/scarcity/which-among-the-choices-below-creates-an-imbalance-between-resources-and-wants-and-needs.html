Which among the choices below creates an imbalance between resources and wants and needs?

(A) Making responsible decisions

(B) ** Doing things that you want to do

(C) Consulting other people before doing something

(D) Considering if something is really a need or just a want

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scarcity exists in any society or situation where there is an imbalance between relatively unlimited wants and the limited resources available to satisfy those wants.

Concept note-2: -Resources are scarce because we live in a world in which humans’ wants are infinite but the land, labor, and capital required to satisfy those wants are limited. This conflict between society’s unlimited wants and our limited resources means choices must be made when deciding how to allocate scarce resources.

Concept note-3: -Scarcity refers to the finite nature and availability of resources while choice refers to people’s decisions about sharing and using those resources. The problem of scarcity and choice lies at the very heart of economics, which is the study of how individuals and society choose to allocate scarce resources.

Concept note-4: -One of the defining features of economics is scarcity, which deals with how people satisfy unlimited wants and needs with limited resources. Scarcity affects the monetary value people place on goods and services and how governments and private firms decide to distribute resources.
