Which of the following is not a phase in the business cycle?

(A) Peak.

(B) Trough.

(C) ** Shutdown.

(D) Expansion.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The option is true as stagflation is not a part of the business cycle.

Concept note-2: -Key Takeaways The business cycle goes through four major phases: expansion, peak, contraction, and trough. All economies go through this cycle, though the length and intensity of each phase varies.

Concept note-3: -The four fundamental stages of the business cycle are expansion, peak, contraction and trough. The National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) measures the business cycle by analyzing quarterly gross domestic product (GDP).

Concept note-4: -Listed in order, the phases of a complete business cycle are expansion, contraction, trough, peak. contraction, recession, depression, expansion. expansion, contraction, trough, peak. contraction, trough, expansion, peak.
