(A) frictional unemployment

(B) ** structural unemployment

(C) cyclical unemployment

(D) seasonal unemployment

Concept note-1: -According to the natural rate model, if government attempts to maintain an unemployment rate below the natural rate of unemployment, inflation will increase and continuously rise until unemployment returns to its natural rate.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Various schemes include Ayushman Bharat, Prime Minister Awaas Yojana-Gramin, Schemes for Unemployed Women, Atal Pension Yojana, Social security relief to dependents of workers passing away due to COVID-19, Support for Marginalized Individuals for Livelihood and Enterprise, etc.
