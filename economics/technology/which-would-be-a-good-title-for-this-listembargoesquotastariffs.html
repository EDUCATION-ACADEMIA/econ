EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main types of trade barriers used by countries seeking a protectionist policy or as a form of retaliatory trade barriers are subsidies, standardization, tariffs, quotas, and licenses.

Concept note-2: -Quotas are import limits that prevent more than a set amount of a specific good from being imported into a country. An embargo is a ban on the trade of a particular good, category of good, or with a specific country.

Concept note-3: -The most common barrier to trade is a tariff–a tax on imports. Tariffs raise the price of imported goods relative to domestic goods (good produced at home).

Concept note-4: -Tariffs are a tax on imports. Quotas are a limit on the number of a certain good that can be imported from a certain country. Embargoes occur when one country bans trade with another country. More items
