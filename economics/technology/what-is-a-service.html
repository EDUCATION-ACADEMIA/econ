What is a service?

(A) A job

(B) Something someone does for you

(C) A need

(D) ** Something you do for another person

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Acts of service: This love language centers on doing activities that make life easier or more enjoyable for the other person, such as running errands, picking up the dry cleaning, doing the grocery shopping, or other household chores.

Concept note-2: -An act of service is the physical expression of a thoughtful gesture. It’s one of the five love languages, which are specific styles of showing love. At its core, an act of service is about someone going out of their way to meaningfully help and support the other person.

Concept note-3: -Prepare breakfast, lunch, or dinner if your partner is usually the cook. Clean your partner’s car before they wake up. Take the kids and leave your partner to relax with no responsibilities for a few hours. Take out the trash. Pick up their mail. 11-Apr-2019

Concept note-4: -Make them a cup of tea. Do a simple repair project off their to-do list. Make a favorite meal. Surprise them at work with their favorite coffee drink.
