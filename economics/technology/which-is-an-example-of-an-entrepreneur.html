Which is an example of an entrepreneur?

(A) A manager of a business

(B) A person who runs a government-owned news organization

(C) A plumber who works for a company owned by an individual

(D) ** A person who uses his/her money to start a business selling a product

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A single-location restaurant, one grocery shop, or a retail shop to sell your handmade goods would all be an example of small business entrepreneurship. These individuals usually invest their own money and succeed if their business turns a profit, which they live off of.

Concept note-2: -Entrepreneurship examples can be startup companies like BYJUâ€™S, Ola, Flipkart, etc, or it can be any small business that is started by an individual or a group of individuals.

Concept note-3: -Spontaneous Entrepreneur: These entrepreneurs start their business because of their natural talents.

Concept note-4: -The term business-to-consumer (B2C) refers to the process of selling products and services directly between a business and consumers who are the end-users of its products or services. Most companies that sell directly to consumers can be referred to as B2C companies.
