Citizens have no real role in selecting their leader in ____ since it is an Autocracy.

(A) ** North Korea

(B) South Korea

(C) Japan

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -North Korea’s political system is built upon the principle of centralization. The constitution defines North Korea as “a dictatorship of people’s democracy” under the leadership of the Workers’ Party of Korea (WPK), which is given legal supremacy over other political parties.

Concept note-2: -The Economist Intelligence Unit rated South Korea a “full democracy” in 2022.

Concept note-3: -North Korea was admitted to the Non-Aligned Movement in 1975 and has since presented itself as a leader of the Third World, promoting Juche as a model for developing countries to follow.
