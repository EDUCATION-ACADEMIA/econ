Empowerment means ____

(A) Control of resources and access to opportunities

(B) Absence of other anti-poverty

(C) ** Eliminating the deprivations and creating conditions of equality of opportunities

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Empowerment means people having power and control over their own lives. People get the support they need that is right for them. Empowerment means that people are equal citizens. They are respected and confident in their communities. You canâ€™t empower someone else or make someone empowered.

Concept note-2: -Economic empowerment is the capacity of women and men to participate in, contribute to and benefit from growth processes in ways that recognise the value of their contributions, respect their dignity and make it possible to negotiate a fairer distribution of the benefits of growth.

Concept note-3: -1 argue that there are three dimensions of empowerment: self-empowerment through individual action, mutual empowerment that is interpersonal, and social empowerment in the outcomes of social action.

Concept note-4: -Social Empowerment means all the sections of society in India, have equal control over their lives, are able to take important decisions in their lives, and have equal opportunities. Without empowering all sections of society equally, a nation can never have a good growth trajectory.
