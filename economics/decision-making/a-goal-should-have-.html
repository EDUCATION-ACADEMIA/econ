A Goal should have ____

(A) Pretty flowers

(B) Big Words

(C) ** A Timeline

(D) small words

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You need to create a timeline in order to properly create a schedule and make progress towards your goal. Creating a timeline takes your goal from something that will happen in the future and moves it into the present. A timeline also creates a commitment to achieve your goal.

Concept note-2: -A goal timeline creates a sense of urgency, acting as the fuel to meet your business targets. Set a clear direction and take actionable steps to pave the way for you team to achieve their goals and objectives with our content-ready PowerPoint Templates.

Concept note-3: -A deadline is an essential component of goal-setting, since it establishes the “when” a goal will be completed. There’s also the “Time-Bound” element of SMART Goals, another great goal-setting methodology for students to learn and use.

Concept note-4: -Goals should have a time frame. You won’t feel very motivated if your goal is vaguely “to finish college someday.” If you’re realistic and specific in your goals, you should also be able to project a time frame for reaching the goal. You should really want to reach the goal.

Concept note-5: -Set a realistic expectation to be able to create a timeline for your goals. Envisionise your goal in the short term and long term process.
