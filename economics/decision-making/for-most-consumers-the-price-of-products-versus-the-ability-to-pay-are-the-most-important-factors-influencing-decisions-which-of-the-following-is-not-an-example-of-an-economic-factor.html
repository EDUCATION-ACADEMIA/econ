For most consumers the price of products versus the ability to pay are the most important factors influencing decisions. Which of the following is not an example of an economic factor:

(A) budget limits

(B) Insurance, legal fees and other costs

(C) loans or credit options for purchasing product

(D) ** trends or fads

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The most important determinant of consumer spending is disposable income. If people do not have enough money, they cannot spend it. Low-income consumers spend a greater portion of their disposable income.

Concept note-2: -In general, there are four factors that influence consumer behaviour. These factors impact whether or not your target customer buys your product. They are cultural, social, personal and psychological.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is C) birth rates.

Concept note-4: -Psychological Factors. Social Factors. Cultural factors. Personal Factors. Economic Factors. 09-Apr-2021
