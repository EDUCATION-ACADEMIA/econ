I will study 15 minutes everyday for the next two weeks to prepare for my big exam . This goal is ____

(A) ** Short term

(B) Long term

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two weeks is a short amount of time, but if you really stick to your schedule you’ll be fine! Just don’t overwork yourself because sleep is extremely important, especially before an exam!

Concept note-2: -A general rule is to spend two hours on homework or studying for every hour that your class meets. For example, if your economics class meets for an hour, three times a week, you might expect to spend six hours a week on homework or studying for your upcoming econ exams.

Concept note-3: -Create your own study guide. Ask questions. Attend the review session. Start early. Organize a socially distanced, group study session. Study the stuff not on the study guide. Take breaks. Stay well-rested. More items
