Things that you would like to have but are not necessary for survival.

(A) Needs

(B) Alternatives

(C) Desires

(D) ** Wants

(E) Requirements

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Food, water, clothing, and shelter are all needs . A want is something you would like to have, but it is not necessary for your survival. Books, CDs, and toys are all wants. Wants make life more enjoyable, but they are not necessary for survival.

Concept note-2: -Reproduction is not essential for the survival of an organism but it is required to prevent the species from extinction.

Concept note-3: -Food, water, clothing, sleep, and shelter are the bare necessities for anyoneâ€™s survival. For many people, these basic needs can not be met without the aid of charitable organizations.

Concept note-4: -A want, in economics, is one step up in the order from needs and is simply something that people desire to have, that they may, or may not, be able to obtain.

Concept note-5: -Physiological Survival Needs: Air. Water. Food. Safety and Security Needs: Free from dangers. Need for Belongingness. Social Acceptance. Social Interaction. Social Affiliation. Need for Esteem. Self-worth, Competence, Skill(s) Appreciation, Recognition, Respect. Need for Self-Actualization. Physical. Emotional.
