Psychological factors that influence consumer purchasing decisions include:(pick all that apply)

(A) ** Motivation

(B) ** Perception

(C) ** Beliefs and attitudes

(D) Marketing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are four psychological factors that influence consumer behaviour: Motivation, perception, learning, and attitude or belief system. Motivation speaks to the internal needs of the consumer.

Concept note-2: -The key psychological factors to understand when considering consumer behavior are motivation, perception, attitudes and beliefs, along with lifestyle. Understanding these factors will assist any marketer in understanding the behavior of their consumers in order to successfully appeal to them.
