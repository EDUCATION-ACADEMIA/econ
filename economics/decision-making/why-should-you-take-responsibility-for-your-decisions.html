Why should you take responsibility for your decisions?

(A) You will get credit when you make a good decision

(B) Admitting that you made a mistake shows strength of character, or integrity

(C) People do not respect you if you blame others for your mistakes.

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rather than blaming others (or “the world” in general) for a specific outcome (whether it’s a good or bad situation), accepting personal responsibility allows you to evaluate your future actions and minimize harm. Additionally, it builds strong relationships and empowers you to influence situations for the better.

Concept note-2: -Taking on everyone’s responsibilities is often a sign of conflict avoidance. In trying to keep the peace, we’d rather shoulder more than our fair share of burden than risk a difficult conversation, or worse, a confrontation involving anger or rejection.

Concept note-3: -Stop blaming other people. Blame is a defense mechanism. Stop making excuses. Accept negative emotions. Act, don’t react. Practice self-compassion. 29-Jan-2023
