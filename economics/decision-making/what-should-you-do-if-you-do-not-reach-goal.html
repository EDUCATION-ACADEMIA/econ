What should you do if you do not reach goal?

(A) ** Examine why you were not able to achieve the goal and use what you learned to help you be more successful with future goals

(B) Punish yourself for not working hard enough to achieve your goal

(C) Stop setting goals since you do not have the discipline to achieve them

(D) Only set easy goals for yourself in the future.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Get some feedback: Ask them about their experience! What did they do when they hit roadblocks, how did they handle setbacks, what did the process look like for them. Open yourself up to honest feedback. Tell them what you learned about yourself and your goal in steps one and two, ask them for their thoughts or advice.

Concept note-2: -What you GET by achieving your goals is not as important as what you BECOME by achieving your goals.-Henry David Thoreau.

Concept note-3: -Write down your goal. Get your goal out of your imagination and on to a piece of paper. Set a deadline. Set a target date by which you will complete your goal. Work on your mindset. Develop your skillset. Take the first step. Continue to completion. Reward yourself.

Concept note-4: -1) Lack of a clear plan. You need to have a specific goal and map out a clear path to get there. 2) Impatience. Your plan needs to be realistic and you need patience to gradually work your way up. 3) Lack of resilience. 4) Leaving it halfway. 10-Jan-2019
