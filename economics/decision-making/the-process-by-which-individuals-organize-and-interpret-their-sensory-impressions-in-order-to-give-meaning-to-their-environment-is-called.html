The process by which individuals organize and interpret their sensory impressions in order to give meaning to their environment is called?

(A) Attribution

(B) ** Perception

(C) Sensation

(D) Consciousness

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Perception is the process of selecting, organizing, and interpreting information from our senses.

Concept note-2: -What is Perception? A process by which individuals organize and interpret their sensory impressions in order to give meaning to their environment.

Concept note-3: -Sensory impressions coordinated in the brain Sight, hearing, smell, taste and touch are triggered in various ways by external stimuli. Sight is a result of the ability to distinguish electromagnetic radiation, and hearing is activated by sound waves.

Concept note-4: -Perception is the process of selecting, organizing, and interpreting information. This process affects our communication because we respond to stimuli differently, whether they are objects or persons, based on how we perceive them.

Concept note-5: -halo effect. the tendency to draw a positive impression about an individual on the basis of a single characteristic.
