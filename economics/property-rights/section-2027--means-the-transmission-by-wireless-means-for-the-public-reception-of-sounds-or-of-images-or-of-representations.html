Section 202.7. “ ____ “ means the transmission by wireless means for the public reception of sounds or of images or of representations.

(A) ** Broadcasting

(B) Fixation

(C) Producer of a sound recording

(D) Performers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -broadcasting, electronic transmission of radio and television signals that are intended for general public reception, as distinguished from private signals that are directed to specific receivers.

Concept note-2: -broadcast means the transmission, relaying or distribution by wireless telegraphy of communications, sounds, signs, visual images or signals, intended for direct reception by the general public whether such communications, sounds, signs, visual images or signals are actually received or not; Sample 1Sample 2Sample 3.

Concept note-3: -broadcasting service means a radiocommunication service in which the transmissions are intended for direct reception by the general public. This service may include sound transmissions, television transmissions or other types of transmission.

Concept note-4: -What are Broadcasting Organizations? As per Section 2(dd) of the Copyright Act 1957, “broadcast” is defined as communication to the public by any means of wireless diffusion (whether in any one or more of the forms of signs, sounds or visual images; or by wire, and includes a re-broadcast.
